History

Everything started on the 3rd of July when one of our friends told us that he had adopted a dog and there were still other puppies without a home. At that moment, my husband and I looked at each other and immediately we knew that we wanted to adopt but were we prepared? Those puppies were expected to grow medium size and we used to live in an apartment which meant having several walks and patience to train him or her, we also had to take into consideration all additional expenses (i.e. dog food, toys, bed, vet services).

Actually our decision was taken without thinking about anything other than our desire to have a puppy This could have gone wrong! , so we immediately got in touch with this person and Koly came into our lives 5 days later.

Koly could not have arrived to our life at a better time! ♥
